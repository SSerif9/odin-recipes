Description

Dragon Fruit-Banana Smoothie + Ginger

Ingredients

  • 20 Bananas
  • 9 dragon fruits (white)
  • 6 cups of milk
  • 4 pieces of ginger
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 0.50 teaspoon black pepper

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Cut the bananas into slices and the dragon fruits in cubes.

  2. Step 2

    3. In a mixer, add the fruits, sugar, milk, salt, and black pepper.

  3. Step 3

    4. After mixing it, add the ginger.

  4. Step 4

    5. Put it in the fridge and serve cold.
