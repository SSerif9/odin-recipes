A Midsummer of Forget-Me-Not

Photo by Megan Bucknall on Unsplash

Description

Lemon, orange and more lemons

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of blood,sweat, and tears of your enemies
  • 2 red poisonous apples from Snow White
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 lemon

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. In a mixer add the apples and salt. Mix well.

  2. Step 2

    3. Pour the results in a glass cup.

  3. Step 3

    4. Add the 2 cups of blood, sweat and tears of your enemies to the glass cup. Stir well.

  4. Step 4

    5. Cup the lemon in slices and added to the cup for decoration

  5. Step 5

    6. Put the resulting juice outside to rest in the sun. Serve hot.
