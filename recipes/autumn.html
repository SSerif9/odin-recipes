Clematis Berry Smoothie

Photo by Sara Julie on Unsplash

Description

Poisonous flower and fruity smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 30 clematis petals
  • 14 cranberries
  • 7 strawberries
  • 5 blueberries
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • milk

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Lightly wash clematis petals.

  2. Step 2

    3. Cut the cranberries, strawberries and bananas in pieces.

  3. Step 3

    4. In a juice mixer, add the cranberries, strawberries, bananas and blueberries, and clematis petals

    Add the sugar and the milk. Mix it.

  4. Step 4

    5. Pour on a glass jar, enjoy it
