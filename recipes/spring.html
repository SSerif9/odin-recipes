Lemorange-Red

Photo by Katrin Hauf on Unsplash

Description

Lemon, orange and more lemons

Ingredients

  • 30 4x4 cubes of watermelon
  • 4 slices of mango
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of sparkling water
  • Ice
  • thyme

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. In a glass cup grind the watermelon

  2. Step 2

    3. Add the sugar and water and mix.

  3. Step 3

    4. Add the mango pieces at the top layer and slightly mix.

  4. Step 4

    5. Add ice cubes to your hearts desire. Lastly, add thyme for decoration.
